Up We Go

Platformer in Unreal Engine 4

Gameplay

*Video taken by a coworker*

You must reach your destination by all means possible. Your goal: the giant tree! With an unusual body, move across the obstacles in your way to reach your destination.

Development

The synthesis project for my Bachelor's degree. The game was planned and created during a session of 16 weeks with a team of five programmers. The design phase when pretty smoothly: we wanted a physic base game. Something like Heave Ho but 3D. So we spitted the tasks into three: the character, the level design, and the obstacles. Once we had a playable character and enough obstacles to make a level, we began working on the UI and the multiplayer part of the game until the end of our development time. Fortunatly, there was very little problems during the development, which allowed us to progress rapidly and accomplish all that we wanted.
