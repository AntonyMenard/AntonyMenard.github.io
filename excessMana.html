Excess Mana

Tower-defense in Unity for a game jam

Gameplay

You are a great wizard (in the making)! Explore the land during the day to collect runes to build defensive towers, because at night, you must protect your magic altar and survive to tell the tale.

Development

This is the first game done during a game-jam, therefore some mistakes were made. We planned the game loop and aimed a bit too high for the scope of a game-jam. We changed how systems worked too late and wasted a lot of time trying to fix it. I learned the importance of team versatility as we didn't have artists, we had to spend half our working force to do the visuals and sounds. The result is not great, but the experience was worth it.
