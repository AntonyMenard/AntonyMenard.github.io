Be Bold!

Side-scroller in Unity

Gameplay

*Video taken by a coworker*

As the character moves throught the level, the player needs to aim and destroy the obstacles and jump above deadly pits to reach the end.

Development

Synthesis projet for my College Degree. In a team of three, we thought of a game concept and developed it in 15 weeks, divided in conception phase, and bronze, silver and gold phase. We followed Agile methods and practised daily SCRUM meetings. We also made weekly review of the game's progress. In addition to our teacher reviewing our work, mentors from the game industry came on several occasions to give advices development and gameplay-wise. A friend of mine joined the team near the end to create the incredible music of the game!
