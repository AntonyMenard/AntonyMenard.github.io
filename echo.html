Echo

Narative driven game in Unreal Engine 4

Gameplay

In an unknown environment, you must escape without being seen. Fortunalty, peoples are here to help you, but they are not saying the same things. Who will you trust?

Development

An interesting university project done with a multidisciplinary team composed of programmers and artists (2D, 3D and musicians). After picking a serious subject to make a game, which ended up being the concept of echo chamber, we tested 3 game designs to conclude that a narrative-driven game was the best option. During the development, it was very interesting to see how the two professions had different perceptions of the project. We made two weekly meetings to keep track of the progress of each one and even had game designer from Ubisoft Saguenay look at and comment on our concepts.
