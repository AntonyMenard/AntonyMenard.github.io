Development

I worked on this game has a Synthesis projet for my College Degree. In a team of three, we thought of a game concept and developed it in 15 weeks, divided in conception phase, and bronze, silver and gold phase. We followed Agile methods and practised SCRUM meetings daily. We also made weekly review of the game's progress to talk about what went wrong and what was done right. In addition to our teacher reviewing our work, mentors from the game industry came on several occasions to give advices development and gameplay-wise. A friend of mine joined the team near the end to create the incredible music of the game.
